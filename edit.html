Javatar - Editing

Access is currently open, username javatar, password javatar. If you see someone abusing the site, please drop a line to me@re-becca.org and I'll revisit this.

To edit maps: Click on the room that you want to edit and then click the "edit" button (which you'll find to the right hand side of the map). Once you're done editing you can regenerate the maps by clicking "Regenerate Maps". This may take as long as 30 seconds. Please do not click a second time or hit reload. When it's done generating it will return you to the map. You'll find that the revision number will have been incremented. You'll also find a summary of your changes on the changes page.

That was like 10 years ago that it took 30 seconds+ to regen. These days it's basically instantaneous.

Things to pay attention to:

  • When you update a wall please make sure you update the corresponding wall in the next room over. The system does NOT do this for you automatically (yet).
  • A "random" facer is a rotator.
  • If you undo all of your changes then the "Regenerate Maps" option will go away.
